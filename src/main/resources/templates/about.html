Overview

The NIIT Group is a leading digital talent development organization and a premier provider of managed training services, dedicated to building skilled human capital and enhancing workforce talent globally. Established in 1981 to help the nascent IT industry overcome its human resource challenges, the NIIT Group ranks among the world’s leading training organizations due to its vast and comprehensive array of talent development programs. With a footprint in over 10 countries, NIIT Group delivers learning interventions that create superior outcomes. The group comprises NIIT Limited (NIIT) and NIIT Learning Systems Limited (NIIT MTS). NIIT’s Corporate Learning business has been transferred to NIIT MTS via a composite scheme of arrangement in May 2023. The reorganization helps NIIT create a sharper focus on its Skills & Careers Business which delivers a diverse range of learning and talent development programs to thousands of early career and working professionals to accelerate career success across futuristic domains including technology and banking.
